Cerberus is a homebrew alteration of the table to rollplay ruleset, cogent.

the goals of these alterations are to:

The main goal of these alterations is to reduce the complexity in making a roll. The plan is to:

  • Remove the distinction between assistable and non-assistable skills.
  • Make combat and non-combat rolls consistant.
  • Reduce the amount of times a player asks the GM whats the challange level for an assist.
  • Reduce confusion about how to roll.
  • Maintain the functionality of cogent.

A seconday goal is to experiment and add new features that turn out to be fun to play with. The intention is to keep new features seprate and optional.
